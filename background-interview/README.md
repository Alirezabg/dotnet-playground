# Background & Behavioural Interview Prep

Questions an interviewer is likely to ask about **your background** — focused on your time at **Capgemini** and **St Mary's University** — framed for the **Full Stack .NET Engineer role at Elekta** (safety-critical medical software).

## How to use this folder

1. Read each question set below.
2. Draft answers using the **STAR** method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
3. For every story, ask yourself: *How does this map to what Elekta values?* — correctness, traceability, DDD, async/event-driven systems, teamwork in regulated environments.
4. Keep answers concrete: name the technology, the problem, your specific contribution, and the measurable outcome.

## Folders

| Folder | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| `01-capgemini/` | Your consulting/delivery work, clients, tech stack, ways of working |
| `02-st-marys-university/` | Your degree, projects, dissertation, academic foundations |
| `03-general-behavioural/` | Teamwork, conflict, failure, motivation, "tell me about yourself" |
| `04-elekta-fit/` | Why Elekta, safety-critical mindset, domain interest |

## Interview tip

Interviewers probe your CV for **depth**. If it's on your CV, expect "tell me more about that." Pre-load 4–5 strong stories you can flex across many questions.
