# Elekta Fit & Motivation

Questions probing **why Elekta** and whether you understand working in **safety-critical medical software**. This is where you connect your background to their domain.

## Why Elekta
1. Why do you want to work at Elekta specifically?
2. What do you know about what Elekta does? *(radiation therapy / radiosurgery systems)*
3. Why move from consulting (Capgemini) to a product company?
4. What interests you about medical / safety-critical software?
5. What do you know about the Full Stack .NET Engineer role here?

## Safety-critical mindset
6. In medical software, correctness matters more than speed. How does that change how you write code?
7. How do you think about "what happens if this fails?" in your designs?
8. What does traceability mean to you, and why does it matter in regulated software?
9. How would you approach testing differently for a safety-critical system vs a typical web app?
10. Have you worked in any regulated / compliance-heavy environment? How did it shape you?

## Domain & architecture alignment
11. Elekta uses Domain-Driven Design — what's your experience with DDD?
12. They run event-driven microservices with message queues. Talk me through your async/messaging experience.
13. How do you ensure independent services stay loosely coupled?
14. How do you handle failure in an event-driven system (retries, dead-letter, idempotency)?

## Culture & contribution
15. What kind of engineering culture do you thrive in?
16. How do you contribute beyond just writing code?
17. What questions do you have for us? *(always prepare 3–4 thoughtful ones)*

## Good questions to ask them (examples)
- How is the V-model / testing strategy applied across teams here?
- How do regulatory requirements shape your release cadence?
- How are bounded contexts defined across your services?
- What does the path from junior to senior engineer look like on this team?
