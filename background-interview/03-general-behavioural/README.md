# General Behavioural Questions

Classic behavioural questions. Answer with **STAR** (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Prepare 4–5 flexible stories you can adapt.

## Self-introduction
1. Tell me about yourself. *(2-minute pitch: background → Capgemini experience → why Elekta)*
2. Walk me through your CV.
3. What are you most proud of in your career so far?

## Teamwork & collaboration
4. Tell me about a time you worked in a strong team. What made it work?
5. Describe a conflict with a colleague and how you resolved it.
6. How do you handle code review feedback you disagree with?
7. Tell me about a time you helped a struggling teammate.

## Problem solving & ownership
8. Describe a time you took ownership of a problem nobody else would.
9. Tell me about a difficult bug you tracked down. What was your process?
10. Describe a time you made a mistake. How did you handle it?
11. Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly under pressure.

## Pressure & prioritisation
12. How do you prioritise when everything is urgent?
13. Tell me about a time you missed a deadline. What happened?
14. Describe a high-pressure situation and how you stayed effective.

## Communication
15. Tell me about a time you explained something technical to a non-technical person.
16. How do you push back on an unrealistic request?

## Motivation & growth
17. What motivates you as an engineer?
18. Where do you see yourself in 3–5 years?
19. What's your biggest weakness, and what are you doing about it?
20. Why are you looking to leave Capgemini / your current role?
